About HostelConnect

Empowering students and wardens with efficient communication.

Our Mission

To revolutionize hostel management by providing a transparent, efficient, and prioritized complaint resolution system. We believe that a comfortable living environment is essential for academic success.

How It Works

HostelConnect uses a smart algorithm to categorize and prioritize complaints. Urgent issues like electrical failures or water leakage are flagged for immediate attention, while routine maintenance requests are scheduled accordingly.

For Students

  • Easy complaint submission
  • Real-time status tracking
  • Transparency in resolution process

For Administration

  • Centralized dashboard
  • Automated prioritization
  • Data-driven insights
